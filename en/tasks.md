# 📜 Daily Tasks

Each day, a random pool contains **10 available tasks**.

- **Active Tasks:** You can keep up to **3 active tasks** at once.
- **Daily Limit:** You can complete up to **5 tasks** per day.

{% hint style="info" %}
Finishing a creature task grants access to that creature's boss. Tasks can also award exclusive rewards.
{% endhint %}

---

## 👹 Dynamic Boss System

When you finish a creature task, you gain access to a powerful, rare Dynamic Boss of that creature.

These bosses have dynamic abilities, enhanced stats, and special loot.

### 🏆 Boss Tiers

Bosses can spawn as **Diabolic**, **Insane**, or **Legendary**, with each tier being significantly stronger and offering better loot than the last. They can also use special abilities like summoning minions and casting devastating area-of-effect spells.
