# Relic System

Relics are powerful passive items equipped in a **dedicated container** separate from your regular inventory. Each relic grants permanent attribute bonuses.

![](../assets/custom/relics.png)

- **Slots:** Every player starts with **2 relic slots**, expandable up to **4 slots**.
- **Unique:** You **cannot equip two relics of the same type** simultaneously.

## Slot Expansion

- **Relic Slot Scroll:** Permanently unlocks **+1 relic slot** on your character. Maximum limit: **4 slots**. Cannot be used beyond this cap.

![](../assets/custom/relic_scroll.png)
