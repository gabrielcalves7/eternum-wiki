# 🥋 Sets System

The **Sets System** provides players with powerful stat bonuses when equipping multiple items belonging to the same equipment set.

## ⚙️ How Sets Work

Items in the game can belong to specific sets (e.g., the **Demon** set). When you equip multiple pieces of the same set, you unlock special set bonuses.

## 🎁 Set Bonuses

Set bonuses are activated based on the total number of pieces you have equipped from that set.

{% hint style="info" %}
**Cumulative Bonuses:** Set bonuses are **cumulative**. For example, if you equip 3 pieces of a set, you receive the bonuses for having 2 pieces *plus* the bonuses for having 3 pieces combined.
{% endhint %}

### 🎯 Types of Bonuses

Equipping set pieces can grant a wide variety of stat improvements, including:

- **Health & Mana:** Increased Maximum Health and Mana
- **Damage & Magic:** Enhanced Damage or Magic Level
- **Critical & Elemental:** Increased Critical Hit Chance or specific element strike chances
- **Immunities:** Condition Immunities (e.g., paralyze immunity)
- **Weapon Skills:** Increased specific weapon skills (Sword, Axe, Club, Shielding)
