# Items and Extras

This guide details several custom item and event systems available in the game.

## Roulette Giveaway
- **Functionality:** Randomly selects one online player every few hours and awards them **1 Roulette Coin**.
- **Mechanics:**
  - A broadcast message announces the winner to the entire server.

## Random Jewel Box
- **Functionality:** When used, the box grants between **1 and 3 random jewels**.
- **Mechanics:**
  - You receive a text message listing exactly which jewels you obtained.
  - The box is consumed upon use.

## Random Outfit Box
- **Functionality:** Provides the player with a random outfit or addon they do not currently own.
- **Mechanics:**
  - If you are missing the base outfit, you unlock it. If you have the base outfit, you unlock addon 1. If you have addon 1, you unlock addon 2.
  - If you already own all available outfits and addons from the box, it will not be consumed, and you will receive a cancellation message.
  - The box is consumed upon successful use.
- **Tiers:**
  - **Common:** Citizen, Hunter, Mage, Knight, Noble, Summoner, Warrior, Barbarian, Druid, Wizard, Oriental, Pirate, Assassin, Beggar, Shaman.
  - **Rare:** Brotherhood of Bones, Yalaharian, Orb, Viking, Royal Eternum, Occult, Ancestral Crow, Celestial Guardian, Demon Hunter, Celestial Avenger, Murderer, Crowblade.
  - **Legendary:** Golden, Elf, Elf Scout, Elf Arcanist, Dwarf, Dwarf Soldier, Dwarf Guard, Dwarf Geomancer, Orc, Orc Spearman, Orc Warrior.

## Special Boxes (Bronze, Silver, Gold)
- **Functionality:** Premium reward boxes that require confirmation before opening.
- **Mechanics:**
  - Rewards are sent directly to your **Store Inbox** inside a special backpack.
  - You can only use each specific box once per character.
  - The box is consumed upon use.
- **Rewards by Box:**
  - **Bronze:** Backpack, Dummy Tickets, Roulette Coins, Eternum Coins, Eternum Ring, and a Full Outfit with addons.
  - **Silver:** Backpack, Roulette Coins, Eternum Coins, Eternum Ring, Eternum Boots, and a Full Outfit with addons.
  - **Gold:** Backpack, Dummy Tickets, Roulette Coins, Eternum Coins, Eternum Boots, Eternum Shield, and a Full Outfit with addons.
