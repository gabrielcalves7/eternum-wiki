# Custom Areas

## Cities and quests

Explore the custom cities of Svargrond and Yalahar. Featured quests include Inquisition (seven bosses and the Ultimate Blessing), Pits of Inferno for legendary loot, Svargrond Arena with three difficulty levels, Demon Oak, and Blue Legs Quest in Koshei's ruins for rare equipment.

![Blue Legs Quest in Koshei's ruins](../assets/custom-areas/blue-legs-quest.png)

## Hunts

Lizard Isle contains Drakens, High Lizards, and Strong Lizards. Speak with its NPC to reach the hidden island after delivering 50 Lizard Scales and 50 Lizard Leathers.

Other hunt locations include Darashia North Dragons, Edron Heroes, Edron Vampires, Edron Werecreatures, Mother Scarab Lair, Port Hope Asuras, Port Hope Spider Cave, Thais South East Dragons, Wyrm Drefia, and Venore Walls.
