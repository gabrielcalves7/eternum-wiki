# 🛡️ Quests & Rewards

## 🎁 Quest Progression Rewards

Completing certain quests grants additional rewards, such as experience, magic level progress, and skill progress.

Specific quests also provide permanent bonuses to experience gain, skill and magic level progression, or loot chance. For example, a quest that grants a permanent 1% skill, magic level, experience, or loot bonus will permanently make the corresponding progression faster or improve your chances of obtaining items.

{% hint style="info" %}
It is highly recommended to complete the easier quests on Rookgaard, including the **Rapier Quest** and **Doublet Quest**, to gain an early advantage.
{% endhint %}
