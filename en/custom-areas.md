# 🗺️ Custom Areas

## 🏰 Cities & Quests

Explore the custom cities of **Svargrond** and **Yalahar**.

### Featured Quests
- **Inquisition:** Seven bosses and the Ultimate Blessing.
- **Pits of Inferno:** For legendary loot.
- **Svargrond Arena:** Features three difficulty levels.
- **Demon Oak:** High-level challenge.
- **Blue Legs Quest:** Located in Koshei's ruins for rare equipment.

![Blue Legs Quest in Koshei's ruins](../.gitbook/assets/blue-legs-quest.png)

## ⚔️ Hunts

### Lizard Isle
Lizard Isle contains **Drakens**, **High Lizards**, and **Strong Lizards**.

{% hint style="info" %}
Speak with the NPC on Lizard Isle to reach the hidden island after delivering **50 Lizard Scales** and **50 Lizard Leathers**.
{% endhint %}
