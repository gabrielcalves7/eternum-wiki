# 🎁 Items and Extras

This guide details several custom item and event systems available in the game.

## 🎰 Roulette Giveaway

Randomly selects one online player every few hours and awards them **1 Roulette Coin**.

- **Functionality:** Grants 1 Roulette Coin to a randomly chosen active online player.
- **Mechanics:** A broadcast message announces the winner to the entire server.

{% hint style="info" %}
Keep your character online to automatically qualify for the periodic server-wide Roulette Giveaway!
{% endhint %}

## 📦 Random Jewel Box

Contains random magical jewels used for upgrading and customization.

- **Functionality:** When used, the box grants between **1 and 3 random jewels**.
- **Mechanics:**
  - You receive a text message listing exactly which jewels you obtained.
  - The box is consumed upon use.
