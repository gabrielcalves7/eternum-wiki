# 🔮 Relic System

Relics are powerful passive items equipped in a **dedicated container** separate from your regular inventory. Each relic grants permanent attribute bonuses.

![](../assets/custom/relics.png)

{% hint style="info" %}
**Relic Slot Rules:**
- **Initial Slots:** Every player starts with **2 relic slots**, expandable up to **4 slots**.
- **Uniqueness Limit:** You **cannot equip two relics of the same type** simultaneously.
{% endhint %}

## 📜 Slot Expansion

To unlock additional slots for your relics:

- **Relic Slot Scroll:** Permanently unlocks **+1 relic slot** on your character.
- **Maximum Limit:** The maximum limit is **4 slots**. Scrolls cannot be used beyond this cap.

![](../assets/custom/relic_scroll.png)
