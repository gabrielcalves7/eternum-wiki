# ⚔️ Eternum Hotkey System

{% hint style="warning" %}
**PvP Combat Lock:** During PvP combat lock, hotkeys are completely disabled for healing, potions, spells, and attacking. Perform those actions manually.
{% endhint %}

## 🎯 Target & Spell Restrictions

Outside combat lock, you still cannot use a hotkey to target another player directly.

- **Rune Aiming:** Runes such as **Sudden Death** must be aimed manually at a player or at the ground.
- **Magic Wall:** Cannot be used through a hotkey.
